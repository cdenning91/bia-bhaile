A little about me...

Bia Bhaile is about cooking at home your own way. It's about trial & error. Some meals that work & others that don't, but mostly it's about having a bit of fun in the kitchen. After the pandemic hit, I suddenly found myself unemployed for the first time; after the immediate panic leveled off I realised what I had gained, time - something I rarely had before! I didn't take to the sea or start baking as much banana bread as possible like lots of people but I certainly took to the kitchen. Almost every day for the past year I've spent time learning & loving cooking. I never really liked following a recipe. I started to realise it was harder to remember what I did or when someone would ask I couldn't tell them. So (with a nudge from my better half) I decided it was time to create Bia Bhaile! I hope for Bia Bhaile to would grow into a community space for anyone to share their recipes. Check out the social links below or get in touch!
